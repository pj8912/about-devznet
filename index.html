🤔 Why Devznet?

About 60-70% of developer population are self-taught, who are good at building stuff and solving problems without traditional credentials but not so good at selling themselves. With the traditional system's requirement for academic credentials, millions of developers get overlooked. These things makes it hard for developers to create their own network, distributed across many apps and websites to contribute value and feel a sense of community.

✨Devznet✨

Devznet is a platform that brings together a comphrensive range of features such as project showcases, job listings, Q&A and more, creating a one-stop site for developers to create and connect with like minded people

🙋‍♂️ Q&A, why⁉️

  • Stackover is toxic 🤢 and ruled by mods, awarded for questions being removed. Theses things makes hard to get started in the first place.

  • Quora is closed 🔒 and not so developer centric.

  • Codegrepper messed up with their reward system giving us a useless tokens 🪙🪙 with which we can only buy NFT that we don't even asked for.

We just want a simple Q&A system where everyone can participate and feel a sense of community. I personally believe that positive interaction with people online can improve developers mental health.

Afterall what is the point of a Q&A site if you can't upvote a question without any credentials. Why someone has the power to delete my question?

Don't believe me take a look at these videos

Here in devznet everybody is allowed to ask questions, like question , post answe for a question . No one can delete the question other than the one who asked it.

We aim to foster a sense of community and support positive interactions among developers.
